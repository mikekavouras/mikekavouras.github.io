C4Q Tools

One of my primary initiatives at Coaltion for Queens is building out two part internal system. For context, most of our C4Q data lives in a massive Google Drive. Within my first few days I was completely overwhelmed.

One thing that weighed heavily on my decision was that Google had already built a robust set of data collection tools. I was not trying to recreate this.

Enter Google App Scripts

I was previously unaware of Google App Scripts. The idea is simple; write a bit of JavaScript and bind it to some app specific events (eg. submitting a form or changing the value of a spreadsheet cell). The solution was to create hooks that would extract relevant data, format it, and send it directly to our servers. This meant that the organization wouldn't have to change its workflow in order to benefit from this new system.
